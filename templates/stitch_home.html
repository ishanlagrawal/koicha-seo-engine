The Provenance

Rooted in Tradition,
Refined in Pune.

Koicha was born from a desire to translate the meditative pace of a Korean tea ceremony into a modern culinary language. We believe that true luxury lies in the space between the notes—the silence that accompanies a perfectly poured bowl of matcha.

In our Lane 7 atelier, every ingredient is curated with an archivist’s precision, from fermented sauces aged in stone jars to hand-picked tea leaves from the misty slopes of Jeju.
